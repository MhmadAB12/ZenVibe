Breath Awareness Meditation

Breath awareness meditation is a practice that involves focusing on your breath as it flows in and out of your body. Find a quiet place to sit comfortably, close your eyes gently, and start to observe your natural breathing pattern. Notice the sensations of the breath as it enters and leaves your nostrils or the rise and fall of your chest and abdomen.

Your mind may wander, which is normal. When you notice your attention has drifted away from your breath, gently bring it back without judgment. The goal is not to control your breath but to simply be aware of it. This practice can help you cultivate mindfulness, reduce stress, and increase your ability to focus.

Start with a few minutes of breath awareness meditation and gradually increase the duration as you become more comfortable. It can be done anytime and anywhere, making it a versatile tool for relaxation and mindfulness in your daily life.

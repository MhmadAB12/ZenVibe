Loving-Kindness Meditation

Loving-kindness meditation, also known as Metta meditation, involves cultivating compassion and goodwill towards oneself and others. Find a comfortable and quiet place to sit. Close your eyes and begin by focusing on your breath to relax.

Repeat phrases of loving-kindness silently or aloud, such as "May I be happy, may I be healthy, may I be safe, may I live with ease." After generating feelings of loving-kindness towards yourself, extend these wishes to others—starting with loved ones, then to acquaintances, and eventually to all beings.

This practice helps develop empathy, reduce negative emotions like anger and resentment, and foster a sense of connection with others. Practice loving-kindness meditation regularly to cultivate a more compassionate mindset and improve overall well-being.

Mantra Meditation

Mantra meditation involves repeating a sacred word, phrase, or sound to focus the mind. Find a quiet and comfortable place to sit. Close your eyes and choose a mantra that resonates with you. It could be a traditional Sanskrit mantra like "Om" or a personal affirmation such as "I am peaceful."

Begin by silently repeating the mantra in your mind. Let the sound or phrase flow naturally without forcing it. If your mind wanders, gently bring your focus back to the mantra. This practice helps quiet the mind, deepen concentration, and cultivate inner peace.

Practice mantra meditation for a set amount of time, starting with a few minutes and gradually increasing as you become more comfortable. It can be practiced daily or whenever you need to center yourself and reduce stress.

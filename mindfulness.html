Mindfulness Meditation

Mindfulness meditation involves paying attention to the present moment with openness and curiosity. Find a quiet and comfortable place to sit. Close your eyes gently and bring your awareness to your breath, noticing the sensations as you inhale and exhale.

Allow your thoughts, emotions, and bodily sensations to arise without judgment. Observe them like clouds passing by in the sky, and gently bring your focus back to the present moment whenever your mind wanders.

This practice helps cultivate self-awareness, reduce stress, and enhance emotional resilience. Start with a few minutes of mindfulness meditation each day and gradually extend the duration as you feel more at ease with the practice.

Zen Meditation

Zen meditation, or Zazen, is a form of meditation that focuses on mindfulness and finding inner peace. Find a quiet and comfortable place to sit. Sit with your back straight, either cross-legged on the floor or on a chair with your feet flat on the ground.

Close your eyes partially or fully. Focus on your breath—observe the inhalation and exhalation without trying to control it. Let thoughts come and go without attaching to them. If your mind wanders, gently bring your attention back to your breath.

This practice aims to cultivate awareness, presence, and acceptance of the present moment. Start with shorter sessions (e.g., 5-10 minutes) and gradually extend the duration as you become more comfortable. Regular practice of Zen meditation can lead to clarity of mind and a deeper sense of calm and insight.
